Addressing sustainability issues through sustainable business.

We live in an era of convenience and throwaway culture which is unsustainable. Waste is a global issue, to which food packaging is a significant contributor. Rather than waiting for governmental intervention or for the manufacturers to develop some sense of responsibility - there is a solution, and that is to consume differently.

We have a huge amount of respect for what James and Georgie have created – in that their business is inherently solutions driven. From the purchase to preparation, each time we eat is an opportunity to invest in ourselves, and the future – and to consider the impact our everyday choices have on our environment.

We see content and commerce are an integral part of business strategy and GoodFor share this view. Drawing from the ethos of the slow food movement, we worked with GoodFor to create imagery that achieved beauty in simplicity while maintaining a clean aesthetic for the brand.
